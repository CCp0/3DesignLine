What We Do

Here at 3D DesignLine we value our customers...

Modern craftsmanship by a modern blacksmith. Our 3D Creator Pro printer works night and day to build your dream models. Whether your interest is in tabletop games, fun aesthetics or in gifts for your loved ones we're your one-stop shop!

This company started back in 2015 as a mini company where people in the locality who needed very specific things such as new band logos and a miniature cow.

Now we have transitioned to a more professional position, taking requests from around the world! Using our 3D Creator Pro printer we make customized products and design models to print for those who aren't comfortable in doing so.

We have won several awards in Irelands national such as the SEA competitions and qualified for the NFTE presentations also.

Just know that when you order your print, you're in good hands.
